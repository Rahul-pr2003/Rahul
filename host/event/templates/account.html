About Our Company

Our 2Rings Company specializes in weddings and a variety of other corporate and private events, creating lasting memories for countless clients in the Malayalee community and those seeking Kerala destination weddings. 2Rings Event Management has successfully planned over 750 weddings, 100 corporate events, 50 inaugurations, and 300 other private events over the past decade, up to 2024. Additionally, the brand name 2Rings Event Management is often referred to interchangeably as 2Rings Events by the company and people in Kerala. 2Rings Events is primarily used as an informal or short name for the company; however, our official name remains 2Rings Event Management.
