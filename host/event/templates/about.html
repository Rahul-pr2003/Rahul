About 2Rings Event Management

Our 2Rings Company specializes in weddings and a variety of other corporate and private events, creating lasting memories for countless clients in the Malayalee community and those seeking Kerala destination weddings. 2Rings Event Management has successfully planned over 750 weddings, 100 corporate events, 50 inaugurations, and 300 other private events over the past decade, up to 2024. Additionally, the brand name 2Rings Event Management is often referred to interchangeably as 2Rings Events by the company and people in Kerala. 2Rings Events is primarily used as an informal or short name for the company; however, our official name remains 2Rings Event Management. We primarily serve in God’s Own Country as a specialized event management company for Kerala events or Malayalam land.

Our Founder

Mr.Ramachandran PC, a former IT professional turned visionary entrepreneur, founded 2Rings Events to explore his passion for organizing events. His leadership and insight have grown the company into a renowned name in event management across Kerala.His vocal ability was showcased in many occasions and wedding ceremonies. He tried to explore his passion with his own orchestra troupe – Valence Orchestra, but it didn’t develop as expected.

Then the idea of ‘Event Management’ struck his mind. The term as well as the event management field was relatively unknown till 2008. This was the inspiration that led to the founding of 2Rings Events. Ramachandran’s interest in the performing arts brings the collective insight of an audience and spectators to 2Rings events, allowing to organize occasions in a unique manner. For the first three years, 2Rings Events was operating without an office setting. However, in 2011, they opened a tiny office in Thrissur, marking their transition into a fully functioning organization. Right now, as we reach 2025, the company has grown with multiple offices in different districts and cities across Kerala.

Our Mission

Deliver top-notch, culturally relevant event management services across Kerala, ensuring every event is unique and stress-free for our clients.We focus on delivering the best professional events specifically to the Malayali community and those outsiders who are organizing events in Kerala. Our mission also includes providing professional event services to rural areas, villages, and small towns in remote or interior Kerala, combining creativity, attention to detail, and cultural relevance to bring your dream celebrations to life. Whether it’s a traditional or destination wedding, corporate event, or private gathering, we aim to exceed expectations and make each event a memorable and professional, stress-free experience for our clients.

Our Vision

To become Kerala’s most trusted and innovative event management company, blending tradition and modernity to craft unforgettable celebrations.We aspire to redefine the event planning industry by offering innovative, seamless, and highly personalized services, ensuring that every event, whether in cities, villages, or small towns, reflects the unique traditions and values of Kerala.
